Counter Picks Guide +
Which hero types beat which archetypes
vs High Mobility Assassins (Fanny, Ling, Lancelot)
COUNTER Khufra, Minsitthar, Aurora, Nana
Anti-dash heroes shut down their entire kit. Khufra S2 creates a wall they can't pass. Minsitthar ult traps them. Aurora freezes them on landing. Nana Molina blocks dash paths.
vs Sustain Tanks (Uranus, Esmeralda, Belerick)
COUNTER Karrie, Cici, Baxia, Dyrroth
True damage ignores shields and armor. Baxia reduces all healing by 50%. Cici %HP damage shreds high-HP targets. Dominance Ice cuts their sustain in half.
vs Heavy CC Tanks (Atlas, Tigreal, Khufra)
COUNTER Diggie, Valir, Franco, Lolita
Diggie ult cleanses ALL CC — makes their engage useless. Valir pushes them away before they can set up. Franco hooks them mid-engage. Never clump together.
vs Healer Comps (Estes, Floryn, Rafaela)
COUNTER Baxia, Karrie, Lunox, Kaja
Baxia passive reduces ALL healing by 50%. True damage ignores healing entirely. Burst the healer FIRST — they're squishy. Everyone must buy anti-heal (Dominance Ice, Sea Halberd).
vs Poke Mages (Pharsa, Yve, Xavier, Novaria)
COUNTER Fanny, Ling, Natalia, Helcurt
Dive assassins reach them before they can poke. They're immobile — one CC kills them. Rush Athena's Shield for magic defense. End fights quickly before they wear you down.
vs Squishy Marksmen (Layla, Hanabi, Miya)
COUNTER Any assassin, Franco, Kaja
Zero mobility = free kill. Any assassin one-shots them. Franco hook is a guaranteed kill. Kaja point-click ult drags them out of position. Camp them early — they're useless without items.
vs Shield Stacking (Esmeralda, Grock, Uranus)
COUNTER Karrie, Cici, Baxia, Blade of the Heptaseas
Karrie passive shreds shields with true damage. Cici %HP ignores shields. Baxia cuts shield gain by 50%. Never pick magic damage vs shield heroes — they heal from it.
vs Dash-Dependent Heroes (Chou, Benedetta, Joy)
COUNTER Khufra, Minsitthar, Diggie, Valir
Anti-dash makes them useless — they can't engage, can't escape. Diggie ult cleanses their CC combos. Valir pushes them away mid-dash. Never pick dash heroes into Khufra.
